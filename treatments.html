Treatments & Products

Below you will see a selection of the carefully curated brands we work with.

Cynosure

Youthful, smooth skin is built with collagen and elastin. This treatment uses pulses of light to penetrate the skin, treating its ageing support structure. The body’s natural healing processes then rebuild the tissue with fewer wrinkles and a more even texture.

DiamondTome

Using one of the most advanced skin-resurfacing techniques available, DiamondTome revitalises dull and damaged skin and benefits a wide range of skin conditions, such as superficial blemishes, to deep-rooted sun damage. This powerful microdermabrasion smooths, heals and invigorates the skin.

Danielle Collins

A beautifully relaxing combination of face exercises, facial massage and acupressure. This natural treatment improves muscle tone and leaves your face feeling and looking younger.

Gerard Cosmetics

An LA-based cosmetics brand, famous for their innovation and beloved colours, Gerard Cosmetics have an emphasis on vibrancy, colour and performance. Their passion and attention to detail is clear in their beautifully packaged products. Not only that, they are committed to cruelty-free formulas!.

CACI International

With an exceptional reputation for excellence and innovation, CACI International has led the way as the UK’s number one equipment manufacturer and supplier of non-invasive aesthetic treatment systems. Due to their continued investment in research and development, they are at the forefront of new scientific and technological breakthroughs. Their non-surgical face and body treatments deliver instant and visible results.

Lightfusion

This non-invasive and quick therapy is sensational to be used alongside other therapies. Using phototherapy, this device rejuvenates and brightens the complexion and helps to hydrate, smooth and energise the skin. Not only that, it boosts collagen, providing a stunning anti-ageing effect.

Gerrard International

Founded in 1992, this company of over 60 beauty lovers, has a portfolio of leading, professional beauty brands, that themselves, have tried and loved and then share them to salons and spas all over the world.

Medik8

Medik8 mission is to help people maintain healthy and beautiful skin. They create beautiful products that you will love and trust, all of which are based on cutting edge science. They are striving to improve their sustainability by working towards all of their pro treatments being completely recyclable and 100% of their products are vegan friendly. A great choice for you and the planet!

Vitage

Conceived and created in the United Kingdom, by skin professionals, this superior and high performance range of products are made from quality natural ingredients such as organic Brazilian Green Tea and Bitter Cherry. The products work to help skin fight against ageing, as well as repairing and revitalising damaged skin.

Sterex

Experts in blemish and hair removal equipment and products. World renowned and trusted by experts. Sterex “partner with esteemed professionals to deliver the hair removal and blemish removal services that restore confidence and help boost client self esteem.”

Rejuvapen

This easy to use and effective micro-needling tool can help to repair deep damage to the skin without causing further discomfort or scarring. It can improve the appearance of pore size, fine lines and wrinkles, skin elasticity, stretch marks, scars and pigmentation.

Aromatherapy Associates

Sharing the therapeutic power of essential oils for over 30 years, Aromatherapy Associates are proud to be able to trace a direct line back to the very beginning of modern aromatherapy. Crafted from natures finest ingredients, their beautifully aromatic skin and body products “aim to take you on a sensorial journey, truly presenting you with all the answers to your emotional and beauty needs.”
