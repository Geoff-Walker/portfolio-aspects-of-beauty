About Aspects of Beauty

A high quality independently owned salon offering a wide range of cosmetic and beauty treatments.

Karen Daubney

I founded the Aspect of Beauty clinic 25 years ago and have been studying and improving our range of treatments ever since at the SkinBrands training centre for Advanced Aesthetic Treatments on a yearly basis.

We have a range of treatment rooms optimised for delivering our various therapies.
